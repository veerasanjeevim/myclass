The Evolution of the Bicycle and car:

Long before engines roared across highways, before motorcycles and racing bikes existed, there was a simple dream:
To travel faster using only human strength.
The story of cars began in the late 18th century when inventors experimented with steam-powered vehicles.
These early machines were large, slow, and difficult to control, but they introduce idea of transportation
without horses.

By the mid-20th century, cars evolved with improved engines, better safety features, and stylish designs.
Sports cars, luxury vehicles, and family sedans became symbols of status and comfort. Companies around the
world competed to create faster and more efficient automobiles.
